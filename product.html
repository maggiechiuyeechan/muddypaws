A great harness

By: Rachelle Chow, September 4, 2018

I love this harness. I've tried a ridiculous amount of harnesses in my life, but this one is definitely one of my favorites. At first I didn't think it fit my dog, it seemed like it was just a tad too tight, until I realized I'm an idiot and I figured out how to adjust it. It adjusts quite a bit, actually, and for reference I got the medium size for my 50lb Aussie and after making it just a big longer under the chest, it fits perfectly. She walks, runs, and jumps easily in this harness. And I LOVE that there is a front clip and a back clip! Very few harnesses have both. I also love the bright orange color, I think it stands out really well against my black and gray dog. Quality seems good, I'm really happy with it, and so is my dog.

Totally recommend this harness !!

By: Rachelle Chow, September 4, 2018

This is a fantastic harness once you get it adjusted to your dog. My dog was awful to take on walks - he is 70+ pounds and would pull my shoulder out of the socket if he saw a squirrel or anything else that needed to be investigated/chased. By clipping the leash to the front (on the dog's chest), if he pulled, he got spun around towards me... which frustrated him very quickly and stopped the pulling!! It's amazing and I can enjoy taking him for walks now. For the record I did try other methods to get him to walk nicer first, but this one is the best, because he is very stubborn. The only con (for the dog, not me), is he hates it when I put this thing on him. Literally freezes and makes the saddest face you'll ever see - check out the video. I promise he was much happier the instant we got outside and forgot all about the harness, it's just the initial putting it on that must make him insecure haha
